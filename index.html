SP SETIA GROUP FINANCE SURVIVAL GUIDE

Unofficial Edition - Do Not Forward to Management ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

RULE #1: EMAIL ETIQUETTE

  • If boss says "我要升你了的", add 6-12 months to timeline
  • "你会了就是你的portfolio" = You're doing 3 jobs for 1 salary. Congrats!
  • Email subject line "URGENT" = Not urgent. "urgent" = Very urgent.
  • CC'ing your boss = CYA mode activated
  • BCC'ing your work bestie = Gossip mode activated

RULE #2: LEAVE APPLICATION STRATEGY

  • Apply 3 months in advance. Still might get rejected.
  • "Short of manpower" = Valid excuse year-round, apparently
  • Your 14 days annual leave = "A lot" according to management
  • MC on Monday = Suspicious. MC on Friday = VERY suspicious.
  • Emergency leave = Better have hospital photo evidence ready

RULE #3: MONTH-END CLOSING SURVIVAL

  • Coffee consumption increases by 300%
  • Excel becomes your sworn enemy AND best friend
  • The phrase "balance不到" will haunt your dreams
  • 带好Jayne = Your job description just expanded
  • 做好两个BU的closing = Double the closing, double the pain

RULE #4: OFFICE POLITICS 101

  • The Minion watches. The Minion reports. Be careful.
  • Smile and nod during meetings. Vent in bathroom later.
  • Your work friends > Your job. Prioritize accordingly.
  • When boss says "态度不行", it means you didn't say yes fast enough

RULE #5: ANNUAL DINNER SURVIVAL

  • "Voluntary participation" is an oxymoron here
  • Rehearsals WILL eat your weekends. Accept it.
  • Dance teacher's "AGAIN!" = You'll hear it 47 times tonight
  • Winning is nice. But the real prize? The friends you make.
  • 1st place, last place, doesn't matter - you survived another year

RULE #6: SANITY PRESERVATION

  • Find your people. Hold them close. They're your lifeline.
  • Lunch breaks are SACRED. Protect them fiercely.
  • WhatsApp group chat = Free therapy session
  • When thinking "我做、我忍、我继续" - remember it's okay to NOT 继续
  • Update resume quarterly. Just in case. 😉

RULE #7: THE ULTIMATE TRUTH

You are not a cow. You are not a horse. You're a human being. When the portfolio stops being worth it, remember: There's a whole world outside these walls. ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ Document created: March 2021 Last updated: Every single closing period Next update: Probably during the next crisis — Written by a survivor, for survivors 💛
